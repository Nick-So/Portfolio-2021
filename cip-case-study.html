Overview

The Problem:

The website of the California Innocence Project is meant to provide information on wrongfully convicted persons, to give directions to those who may be affected by a wrongful conviction, and to present methods for activists and concerned citizens to get involved in the cause. How can we make the website more effective at raising awareness and funds for their cause?

The Solution:

Create an experience that focuses on sharing information through social media, paired with strong images and vibrant colors. This will make the website appeal to the next generation of activists, increase social awareness regarding unjust incarceration, and boost support for the organization.
